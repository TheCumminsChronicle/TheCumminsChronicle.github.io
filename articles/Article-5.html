Independence Day Celebrations

Posted by Start Bootstrap on August 14, 2016

The Independence Day celebration will be held in the Cummins College Quadrangle at 8am on Monday. This is the first time that such an event will be hosted by CCOEW. The ceremony will start with a formal flag hoisting session which will be held from 8-8:45. Col. S.S. Jaglan, the Chief Guest for this occasion will unfurl the tricolor. The students of BNCA and Siddhivinayank colleges have also been invited to join the ceremony.

Soon after this the students of Cummins College will get an opportunity to participate in a number of games like a quiz on 'Incredible India' and a 'Tug of War'. Exciting prizes will be given to the winning teams of these events. The college will provide refreshments for all the students who attend the ceremony.
