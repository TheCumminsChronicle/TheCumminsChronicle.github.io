ASME

About the ASME Student Chapter:

  • The inauguration of the section took place in September 2016, on the occasion of 25th Golden Jubilee celebrations of our college.

  • The student section has thus become the first professional society of its kind to be established in the entire University of Pune.

  • This student section is currently under the strong leadership of Student Section Advisor Dr. Ravindra Ingle and Chair Miss Tejaswani Jaglan.

  • Since its inception, ASME Cummins has organized workshops, college level competitions and guest lectures.

  • The student section exists as a support network for students in mechanical engineering, and as an introduction to the mechanical engineering industry.

  • It provides many services to students which include helping beginning students to understand the opportunities and responsibilities that mechanical engineers have, developing student interest in engineering through field trips and guest speakers.

  • We also aid our graduating members to find jobs through resume books and career fairs.

MISSION:
To serve diverse global communities by advancing, disseminating and applying engineering knowledge for improving the quality of life and communicating the excitement of engineering.

VISION:
ASME aims to be the essential resource for mechanical engineers and other technical professionals throughout the world for solutions that benefit humankind.

ASME CREDO:
Setting the Standard...
In Engineering Excellence
In Knowledge, Community & Advocacy
For the benefit of humanity
