This New Year's Eve...

Written by Shivangi Sinha of SE ENTC

Some people swear that there's no beauty left in the world. No magic.

Then how do you explain the entire world coming together to celebrate the hope of a New Year?

I think, that's magical enough.

Every year we set a few goals for us. A set of resolutions. Some of which are new and some are old.

Resolutions, that are nothing but a reflection of who we want to be and what we want to do.

Every new year brings a new hope in our lives.

A new chance.

And do you know what's magical about the New Year's Eve?

Let me tell you.

New Year's Eve is nothing but a few passing minutes, a few fleeting seconds that become so special as the entire past year flashes beforeour eyes. All the good times that make you want to smile. Those moments that make you laugh like crazy. Those moments that make you feel proud. And then there are those moments that remind you of your struggles and sadness that you promise to not let get yourself down.

So, this New Year's Eve, let's take a minute before to pop the champagne and loose ourselves to music and celebration to stop and reflect on the year that has gone by.

To remember both our triumphs and our missteps.

Our promises made and broken.

To remember those times we opened ourselves to a great adventure or closed ourselves down for the fear of getting hurt.

Because this is what New Year's all about!

To get another chance.

A chance to do something new. A chance to do more, give more and love more.

It's chance to forgive and forget the past problems.

It's a chance to start afresh and not give up.

It's a chance to stop worrying about what if's and embracing what will be.

This year, let's remember to be kind to each other and grateful for this new chance we have at life.

Because, New Year's are not about endings...

They are about new beginnings.

Happy New Year!
