Dandiya Night, 2016

Written by Tripti Chanda of FE ENTC

It was the night of nights, all the gorgeous ladies - because they were nothing less that night - were gleaming like jewels and everybody danced like there was no tomorrow. The 28 of September brought the Dandiya Raas to our college even before the Navratri did.

The First Year coming down fresh from the high of Fresher's party were in-charge of organising the event. After the distribution of all the tasks, the CRs had a mountain full of work in front of them. A fortnight of preparation later, the night had arrived and every girl waited with bated breath for it to strike 7.

As every girl worth her salt was applying the finishing touches to her make-up or fixing the pleats of the Choli, the uninvited Guest of Honour arrived. Lord Indra was smiling on us that day and showered his blessings over us. Literally.

As we watched our hopes wash away, the rain surprisingly slowed down and stopped. Jumping over puddles and avoiding mud splashes we reached the Quadrangle prepared with the Dandiyas. The beautiful lights beckoned to us. The FEs had done a good job decorating.

Inside, after the initial hesitance and the encouragement of the engaging anchors Aniha, Sejal and Pooja, the dancing began. And Oh! Did people dance! The thumping beats and the resonant music made everybody sway. Some of the moves that I saw that day were too good to be true.

After the initial experimentation with Dandiya, everybody moved to Garba which was soon followed by just dancing to our heart's content. All of that along with the beautiful dance performances on Nagada Sang Dhol which mesmerised us and reminded us strongly of Deepika or the fusion number which had us cheering louder than ever.

All the years joined together and soon the Quadrangle was full to its limit. Then the stage was occupied and the upper floors. A night full of love and happiness, it was from another world. The food was just the fuel to dance more and more and more...until you could no more.

At some point, nobody cared who was dancing with who, it was all about the movement and the student body swayed and danced and moved and enjoyed as one. It truly depicted the true spirit of celebrating an evening with no end in sight with an occasion such as this.

As all good things do, the night ended on a high note with the crowd shouting for more. It is safe to say, everybody is eagerly waiting for the next one.
