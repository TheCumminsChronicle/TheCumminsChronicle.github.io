Mozilla Club

Mozilla is a non-profit and open source organisation. It runs mostly by the efforts of volunteers, and each and every volunteer is always encouraged and supported. It's an open source community where we get numerous opportunities to learn and grow. Every effort to contribute in Mozilla gives us a new experience and more knowledge.

A Mozilla Club in Cummins has been established with the objective to learn and grow together. We guide each other on how to contribute to Mozilla as well as organize workshops and activities to gain new knowledge.

Events organized by the Mozilla Club uptil now:

  • Celebrating Womanhood- The OpenSource Way
  • The newly reformed firefox club cummins
  • AppDay Pune
  • Learn more about Webmaker
  • Cummins Maker Party
  • Mozilla Introduction and Cyber Security
