Snapchat Vs. Instagram Stories

Posted by Kanika Narang on August 10, 2016

Who will win the battle of stories? Instagram or Snapchat?
The game is on.

A few days ago Instagram unveiled a new feature called "Stories" that allows its users to capture and share certain moments in their lives.These insta stories can be viewed by your followers for 24 hours after you post it. This sounds familiar, right ? Yes,it is the same as the snapchat's story feature.Both platforms offer the exact same facilities: you can add filters,captions or select a pen to draw on your snaps.

This new update was met with mixed responses.

A number of users were not pleased with it. Many accused the company of not being original.

But the internet is not really about originality anymore. Your success depends on the way you package your product.The more convenient a thing is, the better it sells.

So, instead of judging instagram for what it did, I decided to use their new feature and compare it to the existing "story" feature of Snapchat. And this is what I came up with:

1.You can use emojis, filters, text and colors on both platforms to enhance your Story.
2.People can reply to your Stories on both platforms and you can see who has viewed your story.
3.But Instagram's feature is easier to use as compared to snapchat because of it's simple design and interface.
4.It is also buoyed by Instagram's large user base which snapchat simply can't match.
5.Now we have to do everything twice? Not really. There is a way you can import your snapchat stories to instagram.
6.Instagram doesn't show you who screenshots your story, but Snapchat does.
7.You can pause in the middle of the story (just press down on the screen!) or go back to a story you just saw (swipe to the left!) in Instagram.
8.On Instagram, it's easy to find and follow people and track their stories.
9.But there are two things Instagram lacks that makes Snapchat worth using : geofilters and selfie lenses. Geofilters are visual ways to denote where you shot a snap and selfie lenses are basically filters for your selfies which detect the shape of your face and applies the filter to it.

I have given you a detailed comparison of the insta story and the Snapchat story. The ball is now in your court. You can try both the platforms, decide the one that you like, and tell us about your views!
